Gaborone

Gaborone is the capital city of Botswana.It is known for the Gaborone game reserve,sheltering native animals like

wildebeest and impala,plus resident and migratory birds.In the city center,the National Museum and Art Gallery

displays art and cultural artifacts.De Beers,one of the worlds largest diamond mining companies,was founded near

Gaborone in 1888.Gaborone has all the facilities of any modern capital city.There is a range of hotels,and a choice

of cinemas and casinos.Restaurants are numerous and varied,nightclubs often host live music by local artists.
