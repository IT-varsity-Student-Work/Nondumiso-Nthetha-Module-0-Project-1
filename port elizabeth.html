Port elizabeth

Port Elizabeth is a city on Algoa Bay in South Africas Eastern Cape Province.A major port,which is also known for its numerous

beaches.The Donkin Heritage Trail takes in the Old Hill neighbourhood Victorian landmarks.Coastal boat tours spot whales and

rare seabirds,while wildlife reserves outside the metropolitan area are home to elephants,rhinos and other big game.

The biggest sporting events in Port Elizabeth, is the annual Ironman triathlon and the Herald Cycle tour. The Tuna classic

deep-sea fishing competition attracts anglers from all over the world.The city has facilities catering for cricket, rugby

union, athletics, association football, field hockey and many other sports. Its coastal location also makes it a base for many

watersports
